How It Works

Getting started is simple and straightforward

1

Enable Location

Grant location permissions when prompted by your browser. Your position will be displayed on the map in real-time.

2

Set Destination

Click the microphone button and speak your destination like "Jakarta" or "Bandung". Our system will find the exact location.

3

Listen & Follow

Hear complete route information in Indonesian: distance, estimated time, and detailed turn-by-turn directions.

4

Navigate

Follow the audio guidance provided. Routes update automatically every 3 seconds to match your movement.
